Typical costs include a 2% transfer fee (split with developer or 100% buyer), a 1% mortgage registration fee if applicable, and roughly 0.5–1% for legal/title fees. Pre-sale properties may have a small reservation deposit (฿50,000–฿200,000) to secure your unit.

Pre-Sale properties are purchased before or during construction. They typically offer lower entry prices and flexible payment schedules. Cocoon only lists pre-sale projects from established developers with a proven delivery record.

For a ready-to-move unit, the process from offer accepted to title transfer typically takes 4–8 weeks, depending on financing and FET documentation. Pre-sale timelines depend on the construction schedule, usually 2–4 years from launch.
